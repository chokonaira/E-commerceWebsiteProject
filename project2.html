About Stoubridge Automative LTD


Stoubridge Automative LTD is an automobile company founded by Mr Chuka Okonkwo with 5years track recode in the automobile industry of Kaduna state, with a strong customer network around northern Nigeria. We are a state of the art car shop, which comprises of both a physical car garage and an online shop. We deal only on Mercedes Products, comprising of cars and car parts. We only render car repair service through our team of Engineers to our esteemed customers worldwide. Our Management team comprises of our young C.E.O, who has built this company from scratch together with a team of professionals who man various managerial positions within the company. We are committed to delivering the best automobile products and services as our primary goal in franchise with Mercedes Automobile INC. Germany.
